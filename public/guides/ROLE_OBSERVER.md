# Role: Observer

You watch. You can't act on cards, but you can comment on any card. Take notes for the retrospective.

## What you see

Everything participants see, except role-specific action buttons. The board, the columns, the cards, the comments.

## What to look for

- **Bottlenecks**: where do cards pile up? Who's the slow link? Is it the role or the person?
- **Communication patterns**: when a Tester rejects, does the Developer pick up quickly? Do they read the rejection reason or guess?
- **Sprint-over-sprint behavior**: does the team adapt when hackers arrive in Sprint 2? Does the introduction of containers in Sprint 3 change priorities?
- **Acceptance Criteria drift**: is Business holding cards to the criteria they wrote, or has the criteria become a formality?

## Tips

- Use the comment thread on cards as a journal. The facilitator can hide individual comments, but they're never lost (the audit trail persists).
- The leaderboard updates live. Watch for surprising shifts.
