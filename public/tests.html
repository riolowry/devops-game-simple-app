Test harness

Six categories of tests. Frontend exercises pure logic (no DB). Setup checks schema and seed rows. Unit checks DB primitives. E2E walks full pipeline flows. Sprint mutates real game_state (snapshot + auto-restore on finish, even if a test fails). Cleanup deletes everything prefixed TEST: or TEST- . Keep index.html or admin.html open in another tab to watch the board live during E2E runs.
